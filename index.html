My Story

Early Fascination

As a kid, I loved building and painting model planes.

I remember my father taking me to the 2009 Paris Le Bourget Air Show, when I saw the A380 flying I couldn't believe that something this big could fly, it felt almost magical.

Thanks to my parents, I had the chance to go on a plane modeling summer camp and flew a glider in the Pyrenees during this summer camp — that flight sealed my passion for aviation.

Brevet de Base and PPL

Wanting to start flying I started my PPL training in 2012.
I started training in Chelles next to Paris (LFPH).
I first got the now removed Brevet de Base at 15 years old, this license made it possible to fly within 30 km around your base aerodrome and was a french only license.
During the next part of the training I switched to the Evektor SportStar and I finally obtained my PPL at 17 years old.
